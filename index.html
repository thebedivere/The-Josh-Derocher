I'm a front-end developer in the greater Boston area. I code mostly with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, and PHP. I work with major frameworks and libraries including Wordpress, Bootstrap, Foundation, ExpressionEngine, and Polymer. I am familiar with a variety of tools and software including Git, Grunt, Gulp, Bower, NPM, Yeoman, Brackets, Linux, the command line, Team Foundation Server, and JIRA.

I wrote professionally about video games for four years. I started off with a small personal blog, then I began volunteering for smaller sites, and eventually became a contributor for Destructoid, one of the biggest independently owned gaming sites. I have also contributed to Bitmob (now GamesBeat) and Front Towards Gamer.
